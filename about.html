Our Story

About CodeAcademy Pro

We started with one mission: make world-class tech education accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Real Education

CodeAcademy Pro was founded in 2019 by a team of software engineers and educators who were frustrated with the gap between what schools taught and what the tech industry actually needed.

We built a curriculum rooted in real-world projects, mentored by practicing professionals, and designed to get you hired — not just certified. Since launch, we've helped over 12,000 students across 40 countries launch careers in tech.

Our approach is simple: learn by building. Every lesson, every module, every course is anchored to a real deliverable you can show employers.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Two statements that guide every decision we make.

🎯 Our Mission

To democratize access to high-quality tech education by providing affordable, project-based, and industry-aligned courses that equip learners with skills employers actually need.

We believe geography, background, or income should never be a barrier to a career in technology. Every resource we create is built with that principle at its core.

🔭 Our Vision

A world where one million people have launched fulfilling tech careers through CodeAcademy Pro — regardless of where they started, what they studied, or where they live.

We envision a global community of builders, creators, and innovators who chose to change their lives through learning — and we want to be the platform that made it possible.

Our Approach

What Makes Us Different

Most online courses dump hours of video on you and call it teaching. We take a different approach — every module is structured around building something real, with mentors available at every step.

Our instructors aren't just teachers — they're working developers, designers, and data scientists who bring current industry knowledge into the classroom every single day.

We also care deeply about what happens after graduation. Our dedicated career team works with each student individually on resumes, interview prep, and job applications until they land their first role.
